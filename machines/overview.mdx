title: Overview
---

Komodo machines are virtualized or containerized development environments provided by Komodo AI that run on the customer's infrastructure, designed for efficient AI development at scale.

#### Key Features:

- **Pre-configured Environments**: Includes popular AI/ML frameworks, languages, and tools.
- **Scalability**: Easily scale resources up or down based on project needs.
- **Flexibility**: Supports various cloud providers for preferred infrastructure choice.
- **Collaboration**: Shared environments and version control integration.
- **Automation**: Integrates with CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment.
- **Security**: Managed access controls and compliance with industry standards.

These features streamline the AI development process, reduce setup time, and enhance productivity for AI engineers.
