title: "What is Komodo AI?"
description: "A universal interface to all GPU providers."
---

Komodo is the AI developer platform with tools for developing, training and serving custom, fine-tuned or foundation models on any cloud, at any scale.

By supporting multiple GPU providers (AWS, GCP, Azure, Lambda, and counting) we ensure maximum GPU availability at the most competitive prices.

Whether you're a machine learning engineer, researcher or just getting into AI development you can use the Komodo platform through all stages of the development lifecycle.
