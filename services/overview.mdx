### Komodo Services

Komodo services are deployable AI and machine learning applications or APIs managed by Komodo AI, designed to run efficiently on the customer's infrastructure.

#### Key Features:

- **Deployment Management**: Simplifies the deployment of AI/ML applications and APIs.
- **Scalability**: Automatically scale services to handle varying workloads.
- **Automation**: Integrates with CI/CD pipelines for continuous deployment and updates.
- **Flexibility**: Support for various cloud providers and infrastructure setups.
- **Collaboration**: Shared deployment environments and monitoring tools for team collaboration.
- **Security**: Ensures data privacy and secure operation of services on customer infrastructure.

These features facilitate the efficient deployment and management of AI/ML applications, enhancing productivity and reducing operational overhead.
