title: Overview
---

Komodo jobs are tasks or workloads managed by Komodo AI, designed for executing AI and machine learning processes efficiently on the customer's infrastructure.

#### Key Features:

- **Task Management**: Efficient handling of AI/ML tasks and workloads.
- **Scalability**: Dynamically allocate resources to match the demands of the job.
- **Automation**: Integrate with CI/CD pipelines to automate job execution and monitoring.
- **Flexibility**: Support for various cloud providers and infrastructure setups.
- **Collaboration**: Shared resources and job status tracking for team collaboration.
- **Security**: Ensuring data privacy and secure execution of jobs on customer infrastructure.

These features enable efficient and scalable execution of AI/ML tasks, reducing overhead and increasing productivity.
